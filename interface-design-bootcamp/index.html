Interface Design Bootcamp

Whether you are designing a Web app or a website, following best practices and standard design methodologies will always help ensure your interfaces are usable and engaging. In this workshop we'll explore the design process in detail including user research, defining workflows, interaction, aesthetics, and personality. Through real world examples you'll see how an idea can evolve into an interface.
